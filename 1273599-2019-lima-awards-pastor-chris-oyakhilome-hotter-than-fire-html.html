2019 LIMA Awards with Pastor Chris Oyakhilome will be Hotter Than Fire

 Posted on April 8, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  690 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

The 2019 LIMA Awards to celebrate artists with great talent and passion, who deliver hope and inspiration, instil faith and provide believers of the LoveWorld Nation with a good gospel playlist of spirit-filled music.

The theme of this year’s highly-anticipated annual LoveWorld International Music and Arts (LIMA) Awards is ‘Hotter Than Fire’, setting the stage for a spectacular celebration of the impactful glory of gospel music. Pastor Chris Oyakhilome, president and founder of LoveWorld Incorporated and Christ Embassy, will serve as the master of ceremonies for the event to be held at the LoveWorld Convocation Arena in Lagos, Nigeria on November 17.

‘Hotter Than Fire’ gospel music performances

The prestigious LIMA Awards are the biggest music event in Nigeria and the largest, most spectacular celebration of gospel music in the world. Talented musicians and artists of the LoveWorld Music and Arts Ministry (LMAM) will be honoured during the event for their artistic excellence and achievements accomplished in the past year.

The LIMA Awards are an exhilarating expression of the culture of the LoveWorld Nation and mark the highlighting conclusion of the week-long International Pastors’ and Partners’ Conference (IPPC), where thousands of pastors from around the world gather in fellowship to join in worship and celebration with the Man of God, Pastor Chris Oyakhilome. The transformational conference is one of the most impactful Christ Embassy events of the year.

All the LMAM chart-topping gospel music artists will be on hand to join in the celebration of gospel music, creativity and evangelism at the 2019 LIMA Awards. The festivities will include captivating performances by powerful musicians, unique and skilled dancers, masterful spoken word performers and other gifted artists.

The ceremony will be broadcast across the LoveWorld Satellite television network bringing the divine celebration of gospel music and arts to millions of viewers in the LoveWorld community across the globe.

2019 LIMA Awards expected to surpass last year’s spectacular event

The 2018 LIMA Awards featured jaw-dropping musical performances on multiple stages, remarkable pyrotechnics, expertly choreographed dances and much more. This year’s ‘Hotter Than Fire’ event promises to bring the heat and will be like nothing experienced before in the LoveWorld Nation, entertaining and captivating the audience with musical ministration.

Last year’s winners include rising gospel star and Pastor Chris’s daughter, CSO, who won for Song of the Year, Testimony Jaga, who gave a show-stopping performance of his award-winning hit song ‘Jehovah’, and the celebrated singer-songwriter, Sinach, winner of Best Female Vocalist who has written over 500 inspiring songs of worship.

Pastor Chris Oyakhilome’s Evangelical Gospel Ministry

Through his ministry and leadership, Pastor Chris Oyakhilome has provided musicians and artists with a platform to share their talents and spread the life-changing message of God’s love. The gospel music artists of the LoveWorld Music and Arts Ministry are spiritual leaders who impact the lives of millions of LoveWorld citizens around the world. Their songs and performances go beyond entertainment, they evoke powerful soul-stirring emotions for listeners.

The artists and music of the LMAM are greatly inspired by the teachings of Pastor Chris Oyakhilome and continue his mission to bring the life-changing word of God to all the people of the world. Through their talents and achievements, the LMAM gospel stars are inspiring young artists to go after their dreams.

Under the masterful leadership of director Evangelist Kathy Woghiren, LMAM has grown into a formidable division of the LoveWorld Inc. organization, producing dozens of international gospel music stars and delivering the transformational Word of God to millions of music lovers worldwide.

Music is a powerful agency of evangelism, touching people directly in their hearts and souls. The glorious 2019 LIMA Awards will celebrate the glorious impact of the gospel through musical and artistic expression. LoveWorld citizens across the world are anxiously awaiting the best gospel music show of the year, cheering on their favourite all-star musicians to take home the awards for their hard work, dedication and talent.
