JAMB requirements for computer science

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) conducts the entrance examination for tertiary-level institutions. The examination is called the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME).

All prospective undergraduates in Nigeria must take UTME. UTME results assist the board in placing qualified candidates in institutions of higher learning.

It is important to note that a computer science course can be pursued as a science or engineering programme.

Four subjects for computer science in JAMB

Below are the mandatory subjects you should have if you are interested in pursuing a degree in computer science or related disciplines.

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • One of Biology, Chemistry, Agric Science, Economics, or Geography

Direct entry

You need the following computer science subjects to get direct entry into your dream university.

  • Two A Level passes in science subjects, including mathematics.
  • NCE merit in mathematics and one other science or social science subject

UTME requirements

Your UTME results should meet the criteria below.

  • Five SSC credit passes, including English language, mathematics and at least three among chemistry, physics, biology, and additional/ further mathematics.

NB: The cut-off marks for computer science are quite high and may differ slightly depending on the university you gain admission into. You should target grades above 200. However, if you have at least 180, you can apply for this course.

What is computer science?

This refers to the study of the theoretical foundations of information and computation. Computer science entails taking a scientific and practical approach to computation and its applications.

What is the subject combination for computer science?

The JAMB subject combination for computer science in 2024 is English, Mathematics, Physics, and one of Biology, Chemistry, Agriculture Science, Economics, or Geography.

Where can you study computer science in Nigeria?

Various universities offer this programme. Among them are the University of Lagos (UNILAG), Abia State University, Anambra State University, Lagos State University, Babcock University, and Kwara State University.

What is the JAMB score for Computer Science?

You should have a score of at least 180 to apply for a computer engineering course. You should target grades above 200 when sitting your UTME.

Is physics compulsory for computer science?

It is critical to understand that computer engineering and computer science do not concentrate on calculus or physics. Even so, physics is compulsory for computer science in Nigerian universities.

Can you study computer science without chemistry?

It is possible to learn computer science without studying chemistry. However, it is not recommended.

What subjects will you write in JAMB for computer engineering?

The subjects you must write in JAMB for computer engineering are English, Mathematics, Physics, and one of Biology, Chemistry, Agriculture Science, Economics, or Geography.
