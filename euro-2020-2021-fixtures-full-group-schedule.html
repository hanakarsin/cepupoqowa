Euro 2020 (2021) Fixtures: Full Group Schedule

 Posted on June 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  409 words  |  Enda Jantzen

The rescheduled Euro 2020 finally gets underway today, Friday, June 11, 2021, with Turkey taking on Italy.

Naija News reports that the final is scheduled to take place at Wembley Stadium on Sunday, July 11 2021.

The month-long football tournament would see defending champions Portugal contest for the trophy against favourites like France, England, Belgium and many other great footballing nations.
