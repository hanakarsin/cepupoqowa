Peter Of Psquare 'Dumps' Music Joins Nollywood Industry |Photos

 Posted on May 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  187 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

Nigerian singer, Peter Okoye of the defunct music group, Psquare has joined the movie industry a few weeks after his first singles album, Prodigal Son, was released

The father of two is seen aplomb with joy at his debut in the movie industry as he joins the gorgeous and now vastly experienced Nancy Isime on the set on a Nigerian foreign movie called ‘Shanty Town’

Taking to his instastories to share photos in which he captioned, ‘#Set life’

See photos below;

The newly released ‘Prodigal’ album features collaborations with other artistes such as Tiwa Savage, Simi, Teni, Wande Coal, Tamar Braxton, Singah, Mohombi, DJ Switch, and OvieKelz. 

Peter Okoye had announced in a tweet in January that he was glad to disappoint ‘the naysayers and haters’ as he was set to release a new album. He wrote, “This is more like me trying to express myself (and) my creativity. I want to be in charge of myself. I want to get things done. Getting the opportunity for people to know my own sound. My music is not like every song; you must play them in the club.”
