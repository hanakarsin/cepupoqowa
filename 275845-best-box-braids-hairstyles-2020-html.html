The 1990's were the golden age for box braids and it was during this period that the multiple-strand hairstyle took a cultural direction. While ethnicity may have no place in the modern world, women of all colour have been known to rock some of the most beautiful box braids hairstyles thus they pioneered the creativity we see today in this hairstyle. Box braids styles have also been rocked by some of the biggest stars in Hollywood such as Jada Pinkett, Beyoncé Knowles, Jessica Williams, Jhene Aiko, Keke Palmer, and Tia Mowry to mention a few.

Today, girls and women aiming for a natural look that will help them keep their hair from their eyes literally head for bob box braids. There are dozens of hairstyles for box braids in African culture too. 2015 is considered the revival year for this hairstyle after it was endorsed as a way to protect your hair from weather elements by tucking it safely inside. The hairstyles are also low maintenance and offer high convenience.

How to style box braids

There are several ways in which you can style box braids and make them give a gorgeous look. Buns are by far the most popular seeing they are both chic and hip and offer high variability. Buns can be loose, tight, compact, or even bulky. They can also be fused with elements of braiding or formed into a simple style. Ponytails are also a way to style your box braids especially if you make a high ponytail.
