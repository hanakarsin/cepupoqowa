Churchill Live show comedienne Nasra Yusuf has enticed netizens after sharing a video of herself and Mombasa governor Ali Hassan Joho talking about why she got married.

Nasra tells Joho why she got married

In the shared video, Joho asks Nasra why she got married and emphasises the question by repeating 'why' three times.

The comedienne then laughs and jokingly tells him:

"I looked for you for a very long time and you disappeared just like that."
