Renowned radio presenter Shaffie Weru had indicated his intention to take on his former employer Radio Africa Group for wrongful dismissal.

Through lawyer Nabil Mokaya Orina of NMA Advocates, the former Homesboyz Radio programmes controller will be seeking KSh 21.1 million in compensation.

Lawyer Orina said he would proceed with legal action against his client's former employer should they fail to meet the complaint's demands within seven days.

The choice of Orina as Shaffie's legal counsel generated some interest from Kenyans on social media who have been following the whole saga.

Orina's profile

A brief biography published on the NMA Advocates' website where Orina is a partner describes him as a leading legal practitioner, an academic and certified arbitrator with focus on constitutional law, international criminal law, criminal litigation.

As an expert in the international criminal justice system, he acted as an associate defence counsel representing one of the accused persons in the Kenyan post-election violence cases at the International Criminal Court (ICC) at The Hague.

He also served as a consultant for the Judiciary Working Committee on Elections in the run-up to the 2013 Kenyan General Election and as a member of the Judiciary Technical Committee.

Nabil holds a PhD in law and has published a range of legal topics, including human rights and counter-terrorism, international law and international criminal law.

Orina is currently a full-time postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Copenhagen which he joined in November 2020.

He is also a lecturer at Moi University School of Law. It was at the same university where he earned his law degree in 2007.

He then moved to the University of Turin for a masters degree in International Crime and Justice between 2009 and 2010. From there, he enrolled for PhD in law at the City University of Hong Kong.
