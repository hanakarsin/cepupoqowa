2023: People Are Getting Killed Everyday In Nigeria, Only PDP Can Stop It

 Posted on February 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  307 words  |  Lourie Helzer

Rivers State Governor, Nyesom Wike has advanced reasons why the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) must not miss the chance to regain power in 2023.

The Governor who spoke on Saturday in Bauchi State said the growing insecurity in the country is beyond what the current All Progressives Congress (APC)-led federal government can handle and Nigerians need the PDP to return to power to arrest the situation.

Wike stated further that missing the opportunity to return to power in 2023 would be one offence God will not forgive the PDP if it so happens.

He lamented that the insecurity has made Nigeria an undesirable country has killings, kidnappings, and others go on almost unchallenged.

The Rivers Governor while speaking with newsmen, submitted that the APC has failed woefully and the PDP must return to power in 2023 to salvage what is left of the country.

According to Wike: “It is also to make sure that the PDP is united because, if the PDP makes the mistake of not listening to Nigerians, it will be very difficult for God to forgive the PDP.

“This an opportunity to save Nigeria. We, Christians, know that when the Israelites were in problems, God had to bring Moses to lead them.

“This insecurity, this is not the kind of Nigeria we want, it is not the Nigeria where every day you wake up in the morning, the newspaper says, ’40 people have been killed…’ There is no day in Nigeria that people are not being killed.”

“God will never forgive PDP if it (PDP) fails to take advantage of the woeful failure of APC to take over power in Nigeria in 2023 to salvage the country.

“It’s only we (PDP) that can stop the issue of insecurity if we join hands together to fight to save our Nigeria’s tomorrow,” the Governor declared.
