Funs of legendary US rapper The Notorious BIG have taken to social media to honour the fallen musician on his death anniversary. The rapper died on March 9, 1997.

Biggie was fatally shot in Los Angeles, California. At the particular time of the incident, he was at the top of his career and was recognised as one of the greatest rappers at the time.

The superstar was signed to Diddy's Bad Boy record label.

Various media outlets also took the time to remember the fallen star and posted pictures of the artiste.
