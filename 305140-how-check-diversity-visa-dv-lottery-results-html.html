How do you get to know whether you have been successful after applying for a diversity visa? Today, there are more than fifty thousand people in different parts the world waiting for the lottery results. Successful applicants will have a chance to become permanent residents in the United States of America. It is now possible to check whether you are among the lucky winners electronically.

The DV Lottery has now been automated, and eligible applicants may make their submission online. After your electronic diversity visa lottery application has gone through, you will need to use the electronic Entrant Status Check. This can be found on their website.

Entries for the Diversity Visa are accepted from October to November. American visa lottery 2019 registration entrants have until September 30, 2019 to check the status of their entry. Diversity visa lottery 2021 is also open to those who wish to try their luck.

Who can apply for Diversity Visa lottery?

To be eligible to apply the DV lottery, one must be a native from a qualifying country. You must be a citizen or national born outside United States of America. You must also be from a country with a low immigration rate to the USA. People born in countries with high immigration rate to the United States of America are excluded from the list.

Applicant must also have completed US high school or the foreign equivalent of US high school. This means that one must have gone through twelve years course of elementary and secondary education in the USA or an equivalent of completion of a course comparable to twelve years of education in the USA.

    How much is the diversity visa fee?

    Trying your luck on the process does not come for free. There are several costs and application fees that one is required to pay. The fee depends on the type of visa that you are applying for and your country of origin. The fees that one is required to pay include that of US immigrant, US nonimmigrant as well as a fee for the US diversity visas. There are also some application fees and reciprocity fees. Reciprocity fees refer to the cost that other nations pay depending on the relationship between them and USA when it comes to applying for visas

    Diversity Visa financial requirements

    Before getting approved for a green card in the US, applicants must prove that they are not inadmissible. This is on the ground that the applicant may one day become a public liability. To this end, applicants must show that they have a stable means of support. This can be proved by:

  • Showing personal income or support
  • Proving that you have someone to sponsor you. The sponsor must sign form 1-134 Affidavit of support.
  • Your assets must be convertible to cash within one year and must be enough to support you.
  • Your sponsor should attach supporting documents to show he/she is capable of taking on the financial obligations.
  • A form showing the extent to which the sponsor will go in terms of financing you.

    How to check the DV Lottery results online

    Below are the guidelines on how to check the diversity lottery 2020 electronically.

  • Go to the US Department of State website
  • Click on CHECK STATUS and continue
  • Enter the number of your confirmation
  • Enter your family name or last name
  • Enter the year of birth
  • Check carefully the security code provided and enters it correctly
  • Then hit submit
  • The next page to appear will tell you whether your application was successful or not.

    • The successful Diversity visa lottery 2020 entrants include only those who registered between October 3rd, and 6th November last year. The US State Department will start processing Diversity Visas and carrying on interviews for the winners in October 2019. Those who go through the interview successfully, background checks and qualifications such as high school will after that be granted permanent resident permits. Kindly note that during the peak hours, the US State Department website might be slow because of the pressure on their servers. Applicants are therefore encouraged to keep refreshing the page until it loads fully.
