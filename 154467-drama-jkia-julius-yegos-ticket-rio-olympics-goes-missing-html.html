Julius Yego's plane ticket to Rio goes missing

 Posted on July 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  288 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

- Team Kenya athletes heading to Rio 2016 Olympics, Brazil protested at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport after Julius Yego's plane ticket went missing

- Julius Yego's ticket went missing under unclear circumstances, causing the fracas at the Airport

-Yego was later on allowed to board the Kenya Airways flight, but without the ticket.

The third batch of Team Kenya athletes on Sunday, August 7 boycotted to board their flight in protest over Julius Yego's missing ticket

The team heading to Brazil for the Rio 2016 Olympics caused the fracas after realising the Javelin champion's plane ticket was missing.

The youtube star only realised the predicament after arriving at JKIA.

National Olympics Committee of Kenya (NOCK) officials who were in charge of the tickets failed to produce Yego's ticket for his flight.

After a series of phone calls, the Javelin champion was allowed to board the Kenya Airways flight without a ticket

The incident comes hours after Yego complained about the travel plans of his coach who has been scheduled to travel on August 14 and back on August 18 before Yego competes in the Rio Olympics

Julius Ndegwa, the Nairobi secretary of Professional Athletes Association of Kenya expressed his disappointment saying the incident was a demotivation to the team.

World marathon champion, Catherine Ndereba and Beijing World men 1500m silver medallist, Elijah Manangoi were also affected with ticket problems.
